pre-share checklist

  1. Install a virtual audio device — VB-Cable is free and works well
  2. Open your app's audio settings and set output to CABLE Input (or your virtual device)
  3. In quip's audio selector, pick CABLE Output so it captures that stream
  4. Make sure the app is visible (not minimized) before sharing
  5. Click listen to verify audio is flowing before you share
